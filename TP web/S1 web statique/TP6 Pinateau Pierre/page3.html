With that, the battle continued so long as to make one faint. There was no ground not stained by blood, no sky not filled by screams. The earth was laid to waste and swallowed in dusk, and yet still fell deeper into the darkness of the war of the gods. The “Devil” emerged as a mutation of the Phantasma, and monsters of the Devil’s breed swarmed across the land.
